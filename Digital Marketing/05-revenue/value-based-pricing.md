## 🎯 INTI DARI VALUE-BASED PRICING

Quote yang sering dikutip Patrick Campbell (ProfitWell):
> *"Cost-plus pricing is for products. Value-based pricing is for outcomes. SaaS sells outcomes."*

3 cara dasar nentuin harga:
1. **Cost-plus pricing** — Hitung biaya produksi + margin. Cocok untuk komoditas.
2. **Competitor-based pricing** — Lihat kompetitor + offset. Cocok untuk pasar matang.
3. **Value-based pricing** — Hitung **value yang lo deliver ke customer**, charge fraction dari situ. **Cocok untuk SaaS.**

SaaS bukan komoditas. Marginal cost serving 1 user tambahan ~$0. Jadi cost-plus = leave money on the table. Value-based = capture value yang lo create.

## 📐 LOGIKA PRICING BERBASIS VALUE

### Aturan 10x Value Capture

Aturan praktis industri SaaS:
> Customer mau bayar **10-25% dari value yang mereka terima.**

Kalau produk lo nyimpen customer 100 jam/tahun dan satu jam mereka senilai $50, value = $5,000/tahun. **Harga yang fair: $500-$1,250/tahun.**

Customer pikir mereka dapet deal (10x ROI). Lo dapet revenue yang masuk akal.

### Formula Value Identification

Untuk tiap target customer, identifikasi 3 jenis value:

**1. Hard Value (Quantifiable):**
- Jam yang dihemat × biaya per jam karyawan
- Revenue yang dihasilkan (deals closed, conversions)
- Cost yang dipotong (server cost, tool cost yang di-replace)

**2. Soft Value (Less Quantifiable):**
- Penurunan stress, productivity boost
- Better decision-making
- Team morale

**3. Risk Mitigation Value:**
- Reduce risk of compliance failure
- Reduce risk of security breach
- Reduce risk of churn (untuk B2B yang sell to B2B customer)

**Customer mau bayar lebih untuk hard value yang clear, dan untuk risk mitigation (terutama enterprise).**

## 🧮 CARA PRAKTIS HITUNG VALUE

### Method 1: ROI Calculator

Sediakan calculator di pricing page lo:
- Input: jumlah karyawan, average salary, current time spent on X
- Output: estimated savings per year + ROI calculation

**Contoh:** Tool automation marketing
```
Input:
- 5 marketers × $60K avg salary = $300K
- 30% waktu untuk manual reporting = $90K
- Our tool saves 70% reporting time = $63K savings

Our pricing: $12K/tahun
ROI: 5.25x
```

ROI calculator = sales tool. Customer enterprise especially love this karena mereka butuh justify ke CFO.

### Method 2: Customer Interview Approach

Tanya 10-20 customer/prospect:
1. *"Sebelum pake [produk lo], gimana lo solve [problem]?"*
2. *"Berapa biaya solusi sebelumnya (tool + tim + waktu)?"*
3. *"Setelah pake [produk lo], berapa yang lo hemat (waktu / uang / risk)?"*
4. *"Kalau dipikir-pikir, berapa value [produk lo] untuk bisnis lo per tahun?"*

Compile jawaban. Median = pricing reference.

### Method 3: Van Westendorp (Sudah Dibahas di File 14)

4 pertanyaan WTP. Intersection = optimal price range.

## 💎 INSIGHT KUNCI MADHAVAN RAMANUJAM ("MONETIZING INNOVATION")

Buku Ramanujam yang sering disebut sebagai pricing bible. 5 insight wajib:

### Insight 1: Pricing Adalah Product Decision, Bukan Marketing Afterthought
Mayoritas tim bangun produk dulu, baru pikirin pricing pas mau launch. Salah.

**Yang benar:** Talk pricing dengan customer **sebelum lo build feature**. Mereka mau bayar berapa untuk feature itu? Kalau jawaban "rendah" → don't build it.

### Insight 2: Willingness-to-Pay = North Star, Bukan Cost
Jangan tanya "berapa cost gw bangun ini?" Tanya "berapa customer rela bayar ini?"

### Insight 3: 4 Tipe Pricing Failures
1. **"Feature Shock":** Bundle feature terlalu banyak, customer overwhelmed, pricing tinggi tapi conversion rendah
2. **"Minivan Trap":** Produk untuk semua orang = nobody special
3. **"Hidden Gem":** Feature paling bernilai dipendam di tier mahal yang nggak ada yang beli
4. **"Undead":** Produk shipping tapi pricing nggak match value, slow death

### Insight 4: Willingness-to-Pay Berbeda per Segment
Enterprise customer mau bayar 5-10x SMB untuk fitur sama. Karena value ke mereka 5-10x. **Segment harga lo.**

### Insight 5: Pricing Page = Marketing Asset Paling Penting Kedua
Setelah homepage, pricing page paling banyak dikunjungi. **Treat sebagai marketing asset, bukan operational page.**

## 🎯 STRATEGI VALUE METRIC SELECTION

Value metric = unit yang lo charge based on. Pilih yang scale dengan value customer.

### Contoh Pemilihan Value Metric yang Tepat

| Produk | Salah Metric | Benar Metric |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| Email marketing tool | Per user | Per subscriber/email sent (scales dengan list size) |
| Analytics tool | Per user | Per event/data volume (scales dengan business activity) |
| Project management | Per user (OK kalau team-collaboration value) | Per user (correct) |
| API/Developer tool | Flat subscription | Per API call (scales dengan usage) |
| Sales CRM | Per user | Per user OR per pipeline value tracked |

**Aturan:** Value metric harus **transparent**, **predictable**, dan **scale dengan value customer dapet**.

### Trend 2026: Usage-Based Adoption

Per Metronome 2025 report: **85% SaaS adopt or test usage-based pricing**.

Why now?
- AI cost per token = variable, butuh usage-based
- Customer mau pay-as-you-grow (less upfront commitment)
- Sales motion: lower barrier to entry, expand revenue otomatis

**Hybrid jadi standard 2026:**
- Base subscription (predictable revenue)
- + Usage tier (expansion revenue otomatis seiring customer growth)

## 🚀 PSIKOLOGI HARGA & TIER DESIGN

### Anchor Effect (3-Tier Model)

```
TIER 1: Starter    $29/mo   (entry, limited)
TIER 2: Pro        $99/mo   ← anchor target
TIER 3: Enterprise $299/mo  (price anchor)
```

**Why 3 tier:**
- Tier 1 = signal accessibility
- Tier 2 = obvious choice (paling banyak conversion)
- Tier 3 = anchor (bikin Tier 2 keliatan reasonable)

**Pricing psikologi:** Tier 3 yang 3x Tier 2 = naekin conversion Tier 2 sampe 30%.

### Decoy Tier
Tier yang sengaja unattractive untuk push ke tier yang lo mau.

**Contoh klasik (The Economist):**
- Web only: $59
- Print only: $125
- Web + Print: $125

Print-only adalah **decoy** — bikin Web+Print keliatan obvious choice. Konversi Web+Print naik 84%.

### Charm Pricing vs Round Number
- **$99** (charm) — SMB self-serve, signal value
- **$100** (round) — Enterprise, signal premium/quality

Aturan: SMB pake charm, Enterprise pake round.

## ⚠️ COMMON VALUE-BASED PRICING MISTAKES

### Mistake 1: Skip Customer Interview, Lompat ke Pricing
Pricing tanpa data customer = guessing. Always interview 20+ prospect dulu.

### Mistake 2: Overestimate Value
Founder sering biased positive ("our tool saves 80% time"). Customer interview = reality check.

### Mistake 3: Underprice Karena Takut
Confidence issue. Underprice = signal low value, attract wrong customer. **Slightly overpriced > underpriced.**

### Mistake 4: Tier Naming Membingungkan
"Bronze/Silver/Gold" ke "Starter/Growth/Business" — pakai naming yang **menjelaskan use case**, bukan generic.

### Mistake 5: Pricing Page yang Banyak Footnote
Asterisk * asterisk * = customer confused = bounce. Pricing harus **clear at glance**.

## 🛠️ SARAN EKSEKUSI UNTUK SAAS LO

**Week 1 — Discovery:**
1. **Interview 10 customer existing** dengan 4 pertanyaan value identification
2. **Hitung rata-rata value** yang mereka claim dapat per tahun
3. **Compare ke pricing lo**: lo capture berapa % dari value yang lo deliver?

**Week 2-3 — Calculator + Tier:**
4. **Build ROI calculator** sederhana (bisa simple di Notion atau di pricing page)
5. **Audit tier structure**: 3 tier kah? Tier tengah obvious choice?
6. **Test naming**: tier name jelas use case atau generic?

**Bulan 2:**
7. **Run Van Westendorp survey** ke 30+ customer (lihat file 14)
8. **A/B test price point** (10-20% higher untuk new customer, existing tetap)
9. **Introduce annual plan** dengan diskon 15-20% (file 14)

**Quarter:**
10. **Pricing review jadi habit** — quarterly cycle, bukan setahun sekali
11. **Document customer value stories** sebagai sales asset
12. **Consider usage-based component** kalau cocok produknya

## 📊 BENCHMARK: VALUE CAPTURE RATIO

Berdasarkan beberapa industri:

| Industri SaaS | Typical Value Capture |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Productivity tools | 5-10% (commodity-ish) |
| Marketing/Sales tools | 10-15% |
| Vertical SaaS (industry-specific) | 15-25% |
| Mission-critical infrastructure | 20-30% |
| AI-powered (premium positioning) | 15-30% |

Kalau lo < 5% capture = severely underpriced.
Kalau lo > 30% capture = expensive, butuh strong moat untuk sustain.
