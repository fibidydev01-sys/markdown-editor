## 🎯 KENAPA 90-HARI SPRINT (BUKAN 1 TAHUN ROADMAP)?

Quote yang sering disebut Reforge:

> *"Annual planning at early-stage startup is fiction writing. 90-day sprints are real."*

**Why 90 hari:**
- Cukup panjang untuk **see real result** (vs 30 hari yang noise)
- Cukup pendek untuk **stay adaptive** (vs 1 tahun yang udah obsolete bulan ke-3)
- Match dengan **quarterly cadence VC** dan business review
- Force **prioritization brutal** — lo nggak bisa ngerjain 50 thing dalam 90 hari

**Aturan emas:** Pilih **1 outcome utama** untuk 90 hari. Maks 3 supporting initiative. Sisanya — **NO**.

---

## 📋 STEP 1: DIAGNOSE — KENAPA LO STUCK?

Sebelum bikin roadmap, **diagnose dulu** stage lo. Pilih kategori yang paling cocok:

### Kategori A: PRE-PMF (0-10 paying customer)

**Sinyal:**
- Belum punya pattern siapa customer terbaik
- Retention curve masih turun ke 0 (death curve, file 11)
- Word-of-mouth belum ada
- Lo belum bisa jelaskan ICP spesifik

**Roadmap fokus:** **Customer discovery + Activation**

### Kategori B: EARLY TRACTION ($1K-$10K MRR, 10-100 customer)

**Sinyal:**
- Ada repeat customer + word-of-mouth pertama
- Retention curve mulai flatten (smile curve)
- Tahu pattern ICP secara umum
- Tapi acquisition masih manual + susah scale

**Roadmap fokus:** **Repeatable acquisition + Onboarding optimization**

### Kategori C: SCALING ($10K-$100K MRR)

**Sinyal:**
- Product-market fit solid
- 2-3 channel mulai work
- Churn manageable tapi NRR perlu improve
- Tim mulai berkembang

**Roadmap fokus:** **Channel scaling + Retention/Expansion**

### Kategori D: POST-PMF GROWTH ($100K+ MRR)

**Sinyal:**
- Multi-channel mature
- Sales/CSM motion ada
- NRR mulai >100%
- Brand awareness mulai building

**Roadmap fokus:** **Multi-channel optimization + Expansion revenue + Team build**

## 🚀 ROADMAP A: PRE-PMF (90 HARI)

### OUTCOME UTAMA
*"Validate (or invalidate) PMF dengan 30 paying customer yang retain >60% di bulan-3."*

### BULAN 1: CUSTOMER DISCOVERY

**Week 1-2: ICP Definition**
- [ ] Tulis ICP 6-dimensi spesifik (file 05)
- [ ] List 50 nama target customer real di spreadsheet
- [ ] Identifikasi 3 komunitas tempat mereka nongkrong
- [ ] Setup minimal analytics (PostHog free, file 24)

**Week 3-4: Manual Acquisition Engine**
- [ ] DM/email 50 prospect per minggu (personal, BUKAN template)
- [ ] Schedule 5+ demo/discovery call per minggu
- [ ] Take note pattern problem yang muncul
- [ ] **Target end of bulan 1:** 5-10 paying customer

### BULAN 2: ACTIVATION & RETENTION

**Week 5-6: Onboarding Audit**
- [ ] Define aha moment dalam 1 kalimat spesifik (file 06)
- [ ] Sign up baru dengan email baru, audit own onboarding
- [ ] Wawancarai 5 customer pertama lo: "Kapan lo merasa ini click?"
- [ ] Remove top 3 friction yang nggak critical

**Week 7-8: Activation Improvement**
- [ ] Add progress checklist di onboarding
- [ ] Push "invite teammate" prominent (kalau multi-user)
- [ ] Setup event-triggered email Day 1 + Day 3
- [ ] **Target:** Activation rate naik dari baseline +5-10 percentage points

### BULAN 3: SUSTAIN & VALIDATE

**Week 9-10: Cohort Analysis**
- [ ] Build cohort retention chart (file 11)
- [ ] Identifikasi bentuk curve: death / smile / power user?
- [ ] Pattern customer terbaik: industri, size, use case
- [ ] Refine ICP berdasarkan finding

**Week 11-12: Decision Point**
- [ ] Conduct Sean Ellis PMF survey ke 30 customer ("Very disappointed if no longer use")
- [ ] **Target:** 40%+ "very disappointed" = PMF signal
- [ ] Plan next 90 days berdasarkan hasil

### SUCCESS METRIC AKHIR BULAN 3
- 30+ paying customer
- M3 logo retention >60%
- Sean Ellis PMF score >40%
- Clear pattern siapa customer terbaik

### IF FAILED
Bukan harus pivot total. Tapi **ICP refinement** + reconsider positioning + maybe **niche down lebih dalam**.

## 🚀 ROADMAP B: EARLY TRACTION (90 HARI)

### OUTCOME UTAMA
*"Scale MRR dari $X ke $2X dengan unit economics LTV:CAC >3:1 dan repeatable acquisition channel."*

### BULAN 1: REPEATABLE ACQUISITION

**Week 1-2: Channel Audit**
- [ ] Audit semua channel: source customer terakhir dari mana?
- [ ] Hitung CAC per channel (bukan blended)
- [ ] Identify top 1-2 channel paling efficient
- [ ] Cut channel yang underperforming 6+ bulan

**Week 3-4: Double Down**
- [ ] Allocate 60-70% budget ke top channel
- [ ] Document playbook channel itu (SOP, biar bisa di-repeat)
- [ ] **Target:** 30% increase MRR dari channel tersebut

### BULAN 2: CONTENT/SEO COMPOUNDING

**Week 5-6: BOFU Content Strategy**
- [ ] Write 4 BOFU article (file 17): comparison, alternatives, best-for-niche, use case
- [ ] Build 1 comparison page proper (file 19)
- [ ] Setup schema markup untuk AEO (file 18)
- [ ] **Target:** Index di Google + AI Overview citation start

**Week 7-8: Distribution**
- [ ] Republish content di Medium, LinkedIn, dev.to (kalau cocok)
- [ ] Email lo sendiri (newsletter) ke existing customer
- [ ] Outreach untuk backlink (5-10 target sites)
- [ ] **Target:** First organic SEO customer dalam 60-90 hari

### BULAN 3: ONBOARDING OPTIMIZATION

**Week 9-10: Activation Deep Dive**
- [ ] Setup proper funnel tracking PostHog (file 24)
- [ ] Identify top-3 onboarding drop-off point
- [ ] A/B test variant onboarding flow
- [ ] **Target:** Activation rate +10 percentage points

**Week 11-12: Retention Foundation**
- [ ] Setup dunning management (Stripe Smart Retries, file 12)
- [ ] Add exit survey saat cancel
- [ ] Build simple health score dashboard
- [ ] Push annual plan dengan 15-20% discount

### SUCCESS METRIC AKHIR BULAN 3
- MRR 2x dari awal
- LTV:CAC >3:1
- 1 repeatable channel documented
- Organic content traffic start growing
- Activation rate +10 percentage points

## 🚀 ROADMAP C: SCALING (90 HARI)

### OUTCOME UTAMA
*"Optimize unit economics — NRR ke 110%+, payback period ke <12 bulan, dengan 3 channel scaling parallel."*

### BULAN 1: RETENTION DEEP DIVE

**Week 1-2: Churn Root Cause**
- [ ] Interview 10 churned customer last 6 bulan
- [ ] Pattern reason for churn — segment by ICP-fit
- [ ] Identifikasi top-3 root cause yang fixable
- [ ] Setup health score system dengan threshold

**Week 3-4: Retention Initiative**
- [ ] Implement save offer flow saat cancel
- [ ] Build re-engagement email untuk at-risk users
- [ ] Customer Success outreach untuk top accounts
- [ ] **Target:** Monthly logo churn -1 to -2 percentage points

### BULAN 2: EXPANSION REVENUE

**Week 5-6: Pricing Audit**
- [ ] Run Van Westendorp survey ke 30+ customer (file 14, 15)
- [ ] Identify gap di pricing tier
- [ ] Test price increase 10-15% untuk new customer
- [ ] Refine value metric (per-seat vs usage vs tier)

**Week 7-8: Expansion Mechanism**
- [ ] Implement contextual upgrade prompt (hit feature limit)
- [ ] Build seat-expansion flow
- [ ] Setup add-on / cross-sell offering
- [ ] **Target:** NRR +5 percentage points

### BULAN 3: CHANNEL SCALING

**Week 9-10: Channel Mix Optimization**
- [ ] Re-audit CAC per channel (file 26)
- [ ] Re-allocate budget ke top performer
- [ ] Test 1 new channel dengan 90-day budget
- [ ] Setup attribution tracking proper

**Week 11-12: Team & Process**
- [ ] First hire (kalau revenue allow): content/SEO atau CSM
- [ ] Document playbook semua channel (SOP)
- [ ] Quarterly review cadence formal

### SUCCESS METRIC AKHIR BULAN 3
- NRR >110%
- CAC payback <12 bulan
- 3 channel scaling parallel
- Monthly logo churn -1 to -2 percentage points
- First hire onboarded

## 📊 WEEKLY CADENCE — APAPUN STAGE-NYA

### Monday: PLANNING (30 menit)
- Review last week's metric vs target
- Set top 3 priority this week
- Identify potential blocker

### Wednesday: METRIC CHECK (15 menit)
- Quick pull dashboard
- Flag anomali atau trend warning

### Friday: RETRO (30 menit)
- What worked this week?
- What didn't?
- 1 thing to change next week

**Aturan:** Cadence ini **non-negotiable**. Skip 1 minggu OK, skip 1 bulan = disaster.

## 🎯 SPRINT TEMPLATE (PER 2-MINGGU)

Format Lenny Rachitsky:

```
SPRINT NAME: ____________
SPRINT DATES: ____ to ____ (2 weeks)

GOAL: Outcome konkret 1 kalimat
EXAMPLE: "Naekin activation rate dari 18% ke 25% via remove email verification step"

KEY METRIC: Yang akan diukur (1-2 metric max)

INITIATIVES (max 3):
1. [ ] Initiative A — Owner, Due date
2. [ ] Initiative B — Owner, Due date
3. [ ] Initiative C — Owner, Due date

DEPENDENCIES: Apa yang harus selesai dari sprint sebelumnya
RISKS: Apa yang bisa bikin gagal

POST-SPRINT REVIEW:
- Hasil aktual vs target
- Yang dipelajari
- Carryover ke next sprint
```

## ⚠️ TRAP UMUM 90-DAY ROADMAP

### Trap 1: Too Many Initiatives
"This quarter we'll: launch 5 features, redesign onboarding, expand to 3 channels, raise round..." → **None done well.**

**Fix:** Pilih 1 outcome. Max 3 supporting. Sisanya: parking lot for next quarter.

### Trap 2: Output Over Outcome
*"Ship 20 blog post"* — itu **output**.
*"Generate 1500 organic visit dari blog"* — itu **outcome**.

Outcome > output. Selalu.

### Trap 3: No Diagnosis
Loncat ke "what" sebelum jelas "why". Roadmap tanpa diagnose = guess work.

### Trap 4: No Weekly Cadence
"Saya planning quarterly, eksekusi tahunan." → **Mati di week 3** karena lupa.

### Trap 5: No Buffer
Plan 100% timeline = 0% buffer. Slip 1 week = entire roadmap broken.

**Fix:** Plan 70-80% capacity. Sisanya untuk **emergent priority** + **bug fix** + **rest**.

## 🛠️ SARAN EKSEKUSI

**Hari ini (1 jam):**
1. **Pilih stage lo** (A/B/C/D)
2. **Pick 1 outcome utama** untuk 90 hari ke depan
3. **Pilih max 3 supporting initiative**
4. **Set weekly cadence** di calendar

**Week 1:**
5. **Baseline metric** yang akan lo improve
6. **Document hipotesis** kenapa initiative-mu akan work
7. **Setup tracking** untuk measure progress

**Tiap 2 minggu:**
8. **Sprint review** (apa yang work / nggak work)
9. **Adjust plan** kalau perlu (jangan rigid)
10. **Communicate progress** ke team (kalau ada)

**Akhir 90 hari:**
11. **Post-mortem** honest
12. **Plan next 90 days** berdasarkan finding
13. **Compound learning** ke pack documentation lo sendiri
