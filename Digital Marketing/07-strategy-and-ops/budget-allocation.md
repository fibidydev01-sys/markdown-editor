# 26. BUDGET ALLOCATION
## Cara Alokasi Marketing Budget Tiap Stage Tanpa Bakar Duit Sia-sia

**📚 Sumber:** Brian Balfour "Four Fits Framework" essays, First Page Sage CAC by channel data 2024, OpenView SaaS Benchmark, Andrew Chen "Law of Shitty Clickthroughs", Tomasz Tunguz on SaaS spending

---

## 🎯 PRINSIP DASAR ALOKASI

Quote yang sering disebut Brian Balfour:

> *"The biggest mistake early-stage founders make is allocating budget like a Fortune 500 company — diversified across 8 channels with no depth in any."*

**Aturan emas alokasi budget:**

1. **Stage matters lebih dari kategori industri.** Pre-revenue ≠ $1M ARR ≠ $10M ARR.
2. **Konsentrasi > diversifikasi** di awal. Kuasai 1-2 channel sebelum tambah ke-3.
3. **Compounding channel > non-compounding.** SEO/content jangka panjang > paid ads kalau capital terbatas.
4. **Track CAC per channel**, bukan blended. Channel insight dilematian kalau blended.
5. **Founder time = real budget.** Lo cuma punya 60-80 jam/minggu maksimal. Spend itu kayak duit.

## 📊 BENCHMARK INDUSTRI: BERAPA % REVENUE UNTUK MARKETING?

Per Bessemer + KeyBanc 2024 data:

| Stage ARR | Median S&M Spend / Revenue |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| **Pre-revenue / <$100K** | N/A (revenue belum ada) |
| **$100K - $1M** | 50-100%+ (heavy invest in growth) |
| **$1M - $5M** | 50-80% |
| **$5M - $20M** | 40-60% |
| **$20M - $50M** | 35-50% |
| **$50M+** | 30-40% |
| **Mature public SaaS** | 20-35% |

**Catatan penting:** Angka di atas adalah **total S&M** termasuk salaries. Kalau lo solo/lean tim, allocate based on **cash available** lebih realistis.

## 🏗️ ALOKASI PER STAGE

### STAGE 1: PRE-REVENUE (Bulan 0-6, 0-10 Customer)

**Total budget realistis:** $0 - $1,000/bulan (mostly tools)

**Allocation:**

| Category | % | Examples |
|----------|---|----------|
| **Tools (subscriptions)** | 60% | Email marketing, analytics, design tool |
| **Manual outreach** | 30% | Cold email tool, LinkedIn Sales Nav |
| **Content (1-2 piece)** | 10% | Maybe 1 freelance writer engagement |

**ZERO ALLOCATION:**
- ❌ Paid ads (NOL. Don't even start.)
- ❌ Agency
- ❌ Brand marketing
- ❌ PR

**Why:** Pre-PMF, **founder time = primary channel**. Lo harus DM 20 orang/minggu, do customer interview, ship product. **Bakar duit ke iklan = anti-pattern fatal.**

**Aturan stage 1:** 100% effort di **manual acquisition** (file 02, file 20).

---

### STAGE 2: EARLY TRACTION (Bulan 6-18, $1K-$10K MRR)

**Total budget realistis:** $1K-$5K/bulan

**Allocation:**

| Category | % | Examples |
|----------|---|----------|
| **Content/SEO (compounding)** | 40% | Freelance writer, SEO tool (Ahrefs), content production |
| **Tools** | 25% | Analytics (PostHog), email (Customer.io), CRM |
| **Manual outreach** | 20% | Cold email tool (Smartlead), LinkedIn |
| **Community presence** | 10% | Komunitas membership, sponsorship kecil |
| **Test paid (carefully)** | 5% | Max $500/bulan tester, KILL kalau ngga work 3 bulan |

**Key principle:** Mulai bangun **compounding asset** (SEO content). Effort sekarang = hasil 6-12 bulan ke depan.

**⚠️ Warning:** Jangan loncat ke paid ads kalau CAC blended lo masih tinggi. Fix unit economics dulu.

---

### STAGE 3: SCALING (Bulan 18-36, $10K-$100K MRR)

**Total budget realistis:** $5K-$50K/bulan

**Allocation:**

| Category | % | Examples |
|----------|---|----------|
| **Content/SEO scale** | 30% | Full-time content hire atau retainer agency |
| **Paid ads (BOFU only)** | 25% | Google Ads BOFU keyword, retargeting |
| **Sales / outbound** | 20% | SDR salary (mid-market focus) |
| **Tools** | 10% | Enterprise tier analytics, CRM, automation |
| **Community / events** | 8% | Conference speaking, sponsorship |
| **Brand / PR (test)** | 5% | PR agency 3-bulan trial, podcast guesting |
| **Experiments** | 2% | New channel trial |

**Key principle:** Channel yang **proven** dapat budget lebih besar. Channel **eksperimental** budget kecil (<10%).

---

### STAGE 4: GROWTH ($100K+ MRR)

**Total budget realistis:** $50K-$500K+/bulan

**Allocation (multi-channel mature):**

| Category | % | Examples |
|----------|---|----------|
| **Salaries (marketing/sales team)** | 40-50% | VP Marketing, content team, sales team |
| **Paid ads** | 15-20% | Multi-channel: Search, LinkedIn, retargeting |
| **Content/SEO ops** | 10-15% | Editorial team, technical SEO, programmatic |
| **Events / sponsorship** | 5-10% | Major conference, custom events |
| **Tools/martech stack** | 5-10% | Enterprise tools, attribution, BI |
| **Brand/PR** | 3-5% | Ongoing PR retainer, thought leadership |
| **Experiments** | 5% | New channels, partnerships, etc. |

**Key principle:** Channel **mix** mature. Tidak lagi 1-channel dependent. Setiap channel ada owner + accountable target.

## 🎯 CHANNEL PRIORITIZATION FRAMEWORK

Brian Balfour's Bullseye Framework (adapted dari Traction by Gabriel Weinberg):

### Step 1: BRAINSTORM ALL CHANNEL
List semua kemungkinan channel:
- Organic search (SEO)
- Content marketing
- Email marketing
- Cold outbound
- Paid search (Google Ads)
- Paid social (FB, LinkedIn, X)
- Display ads
- Affiliate / referral
- PR / media outreach
- Speaking engagement
- Sponsorship
- Partnership / integration
- Community building
- Viral / referral product features
- Product Hunt / directory listing
- Influencer
- Trade show / event

### Step 2: RANK BY 3 KRITERIA
Untuk tiap channel, score 1-5:
- **Reach:** Berapa banyak ICP lo ada di sini?
- **Cost:** CAC realistic di channel ini?
- **Difficulty:** Susah lo execute right?

### Step 3: PILIH 3 TOP CHANNEL
Top 3 score → "outer ring"
Pick **TOP 1 atau 2** untuk **focus penuh** (60-80% budget)
Sisanya = experiment kecil (5-10% budget tiap)

### Step 4: TEST DAN MEASURE
Untuk top channel: **commit 90 hari**.
Cek CAC, conversion rate, ROI after 90 days.
Worth scale → channel jadi "bullseye" → 50%+ budget.
Tidak worth → kill, move on.

## 💰 CAC PER CHANNEL — DATA REAL

First Page Sage 2024 + industry compilation:

| Channel | Median CAC B2B SaaS | Best For Stage |
|---------|---------------------|----------------|
| **Word-of-mouth / Referral** | $0-$50 | All stages |
| **Organic SEO** | $147 | Stage 2+ (butuh time compound) |
| **Content Marketing** | $250 | Stage 2+ |
| **Outbound Email** | $315 | All stages (lean) |
| **Webinar** | $356 | Stage 3+ |
| **Paid Search (BOFU)** | $370 | Stage 3+ |
| **LinkedIn Ads** | $447 | Stage 3+ high-ACV |
| **Display Ads** | $551 | Stage 4+ only |
| **Event Marketing** | $811 | Stage 4+ |

**Insight:** Beda channel = beda CAC, beda payback timing.

- **Organic SEO**: CAC murah tapi take 6-12 bulan kelihatan
- **Paid search**: CAC mahal tapi traffic instant
- **Referral**: CAC near-zero tapi butuh existing customer base

**Trade-off:** Lo butuh **mix** — short-term channel (paid) yang nutup operasional, long-term channel (organic) yang compound jadi competitive moat.

## 🚀 80/20 RULE FOUNDER BUDGET

Untuk solo founder atau bootstrap stage 1-2:

**80% effort:**
- **40%** — Customer development (interview, manual acquisition, onboarding)
- **40%** — Compounding asset (content/SEO, community presence)

**20% effort:**
- **10%** — Paid tests (very small, $200-$500/bulan max)
- **10%** — Operasional (tools, admin, analytics review)

**ZERO time untuk:**
- ❌ Brand campaign tanpa direct response goal
- ❌ Multi-channel paid sebelum ada PMF
- ❌ Vanity collaborations / podcast tour tanpa convert plan

## 🛠️ EXAMPLE ALOKASI 12-BULAN (Bootstrap $5K MRR Start)

**Total annual budget: $30,000** (rough $2,500/bulan average)

| Bulan | Focus | Budget | Notes |
|-------|-------|--------|-------|
| **1-2** | Foundation + content | $1,500/bln | Setup tools, 4 BOFU article, content writer |
| **3-4** | Outbound + SEO | $2,000/bln | Cold email tool, SEO tool, 4 more articles |
| **5-6** | Test 1 paid channel | $3,000/bln | Add $500 Google Ads BOFU test |
| **7-8** | Scale winning channels | $3,500/bln | Double down ke channel yang work |
| **9-10** | Add community/event | $3,000/bln | Conference attendance, community sponsor |
| **11-12** | Optimize + plan next year | $2,500/bln | Year-end optimization, planning |

**Total 12 bulan:** ~$32,000
**Target customer acquired:** 80-150 (CAC ~$200-400 blended)
**Expected MRR end of year:** $20K-30K (kalau retention OK)

## ⚠️ ANTI-PATTERN UMUM

### ❌ "Saya alokasi 5K untuk Facebook Ads bulan ini"
B2B SaaS jarang work via FB Ads broad. Boncos.

### ❌ "Hire agency $5K/bulan tanpa ICP jelas"
Agency butuh lo brief jelas. Vague brief = vague hasil.

### ❌ "Marketing budget 50% buat brand"
Pre-PMF? Useless. Brand build setelah revenue stable.

### ❌ "Test 5 paid channel bersamaan dengan $500 each"
Spread terlalu thin. Tiap channel butuh statistical significant data (min 50-100 conversions) → $500 ngga cukup di channel manapun.

### ❌ "Kill channel setelah 1 bulan"
Channel butuh 3-6 bulan untuk fair test. Apalagi SEO (6-12 bulan).

### ❌ "Track CAC tanpa LTV"
CAC tinggi OK kalau LTV tinggi (enterprise). CAC rendah belum tentu bagus (SMB churn tinggi). **Selalu pair.**

## 🎯 DECISION FRAMEWORK: KAPAN ADD CHANNEL BARU?

Sebelum tambah channel baru, jawab 5 pertanyaan:

1. **Existing channel udah maxed-out?** Kalau Channel A masih bisa scale 2x dengan budget lebih, jangan add Channel B.

2. **CAC payback Channel A <12 bulan?** Channel A solid → bisa expand. Belum solid → fix dulu.

3. **Bandwidth tim ada?** Tiap channel butuh ownership. No ownership = channel mati.

4. **Capital reservasi 3-6 bulan?** Channel baru butuh test budget. Pastikan ada runway.

5. **Hipotesis spesifik kenapa channel ini work untuk ICP lo?** Bukan "let's try X karena trending."

Kalau 5 ya → proceed. Kurang dari 5 → tunda.

## 🛠️ SARAN EKSEKUSI UNTUK SAAS LO

**Week 1:**
1. **Audit budget aktual:** berapa lo spend bulan lalu? Breakdown per channel.
2. **Hitung CAC per channel** (bukan blended). Identify yang paling efficient.
3. **Hitung % S&M / revenue** lo. Compare ke benchmark stage.

**Bulan ini:**
4. **Reallocate budget** ke channel terkuat (top 1-2). Cut channel underperformer.
5. **Set 90-day test budget** untuk 1 channel baru (kalau ada bandwidth).
6. **Document expected ROI** tiap channel — accountability.

**Quarterly:**
7. **Channel review:** apakah CAC trend per channel naik atau turun?
8. **Re-allocate** berdasarkan winner & loser.
9. **Kill** channel yang 6 bulan ngga delivers.

**Annually:**
10. **Strategic budget planning** untuk year ahead, by stage of business.
11. **Compound channel investment** (SEO, content) di-bias karena compounding factor.

## ❓ PERTANYAAN REFLEKSI

1. Berapa % revenue lo dialokasi ke S&M? Sesuai stage atau over/under?
2. Top 1 channel lo apa? Berapa % budget? Apakah masih bisa scale 2x?
3. Channel apa yang lo punya feeling "should work" tapi belum di-test dengan budget cukup?
4. Kalau lo ditambah $10K bulan ini, lo allocate ke mana? Kenapa?

**Aturan emas:** Budget allocation bukan one-time decision. Re-evaluate quarterly. Kalau lo set & forget, lo bakar duit.
