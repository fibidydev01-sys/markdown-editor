## 🎯 KENAPA BENCHMARK PENTING

Tanpa benchmark, lo nggak tau:
- Apakah 25% activation rate lo bagus atau jelek
- Apakah lo lebih cepet atau lebih lambat dari industri
- Apakah lo harus invest lebih ke onboarding atau ke channel

Tapi **hati-hati pake benchmark:** itu cuma rata-rata. Konteks lo bisa beda. Pakai sebagai **direction**, bukan **truth**.

---

## 📊 ACTIVATION RATE BENCHMARK (OpenView 2026)

**Definisi konsisten:** % new signup yang reach pre-defined activation event dalam timeframe yang ditentukan (biasanya 14-30 hari).

| Tipe Produk | Median Activation Rate | Top Quartile |
|-------------|------------------------|--------------|
| **Single-user products** (productivity tools individual) | 40% | 60%+ |
| **Multi-user/team products** (collaboration) | 20% | 35%+ |
| **Browser extensions** | 50% | 70%+ |
| **Mobile apps (B2C)** | 25% | 45%+ |
| **B2B SaaS (general)** | 25-30% | 45-55% |
| **PLG SaaS (freemium)** | 30-40% | 60%+ |
| **Sales-led SaaS** | 50-60% (post-demo) | 75%+ |

### Kenapa Multi-User Activation Lebih Rendah?

Activation multi-user butuh **2 user** complete action. Setiap step kehilangan user. Jadi natural rate-nya lebih rendah.

**Insight:** Optimisasi single-user activation di multi-user product first (e.g., signup user bisa standalone explore), lalu push invite teammate sebagai expansion.

## ⏱️ TIME-TO-VALUE BENCHMARK

Dari ChartMogul + ProfitWell 2026 study:

| TTV | % Industri | Predicted Month-12 Retention |
|-----|------------|------------------------------|
| **<5 menit** (instant value) | 10% | 85%+ |
| **5 menit - 24 jam** | 25% | 75-85% |
| **24 jam - 7 hari** | 35% | 60-75% |
| **7-14 hari** | 20% | 45-60% |
| **>30 hari** | 10% | 35-50% |

**Top quartile SaaS hits first value in 5-9 days.**
**Median hits in 18-24 days.**

**Gap antara top-quartile dan median bukan acquisition — it's onboarding.**

## 📈 CONVERSION RATE BENCHMARK (Trial → Paid)

Dari Mixpanel + ProductLed data:

| Model | Trial-to-Paid Median | Top Quartile |
|-------|----------------------|--------------|
| **Free trial 14 hari** | 15-20% | 25-30% |
| **Free trial 7 hari** | 10-15% | 20-25% |
| **Freemium** | 2-5% (free→paid) | 8-12% |
| **Demo-required (sales-led)** | 30-40% (demo→won) | 50%+ |
| **Reverse trial** (start premium → free if no convert) | 25-35% | 45%+ |

**Insight:** Reverse trial trend rising (Lemon Squeezy, Linear) — start full feature, downgrade ke free kalau ngga upgrade. Konversi lebih tinggi karena urgency + sunk cost.

## 🔄 RETENTION BENCHMARK (Net Revenue Retention)

Per ChartMogul 2,500+ company analysis dan OpenView 2026:

| NRR Level | Klasifikasi |
|-----------|-------------|
| **<90%** | Bocor, urgent fix |
| **90-100%** | Survival mode (acquisition harus tutup churn) |
| **100-110%** | Healthy (growth dari existing customer offset churn) |
| **110-125%** | Strong (top half industri) |
| **125%+** | Elite (top 10%) |

**Top quartile B2B SaaS NRR: 113%.**
**Bottom quartile: 98%.**
**Median: 105%.**

### NRR Bervariasi by ARPA (Annual Revenue Per Account)

ChartMogul insight:
- **Sub-$25 ARPA** (very small SMB): Hanya 2% company achieve negative churn
- **$500+ ARPA** (mid-market+): Hampir 50% company achieve negative churn

**Translasi:** Kalau ARPA lo rendah, pricing strategy lo harus accommodate high churn rate dengan low CAC channel. Atau, move upmarket.

## 💰 LTV : CAC RATIO BENCHMARK

Dari Benchmarkit + KeyBanc 2024 survey:

| LTV:CAC | Klasifikasi |
|---------|-------------|
| **<1:1** | Kebakar duit, bisnis ngga sustainable |
| **1:1 - 2:1** | Marginal, butuh perbaikan |
| **3:1** | Standar industri (target minimum) |
| **3:1 - 5:1** | Healthy |
| **>5:1** | Excellent (atau lo under-invest di growth) |

**Median industri 2024: 3.6:1**

⚠️ **Trap umum:** LTV calculation sering over-estimate karena overlook churn rate properly. Use **discount factor** (LTV = ARPU × Gross Margin / Churn).

## ⚠️ CARA PAKAI BENCHMARK INI YANG BENAR

**DON'T:**
- ❌ Compare diri lo ke benchmark agresif tanpa konteks
- ❌ Pakai benchmark sebagai target kaku (target lo harus konteks-specific)
- ❌ Ignore benchmark karena "kita beda"

**DO:**
- ✅ Pakai benchmark sebagai **direction**, tau apakah lo above/below industri
- ✅ Bandingkan ke segment yang **paling mirip** (SMB benchmark vs Enterprise benchmark)
- ✅ Re-cek benchmark tiap 6 bulan — industri berubah cepat
